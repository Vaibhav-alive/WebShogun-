Building the Future An Introduction to Smart Cities

A Intro to smart city

A smart city is an urban area that leverages technology and data-driven solutions to enhance the quality of life for its residents, optimize resource use, and improve city operations. It integrates advanced technologies like the Internet of Things (IoT), artificial intelligence (AI), and big data to create efficient systems for transportation, energy, water, healthcare, governance, and more.
